# Unit Conversion (Inch and Metric)

SmartStitch embroidery machines support different measurement systems to meet the needs of users in different regions.

You can switch between:

- **Inch (Imperial)** — commonly used in the United States
- **Metric (mm/cm)** — commonly used in most other countries

Changing the unit setting affects how embroidery sizes, hoop dimensions, and other measurements are displayed on the machine.

## How to Change Measurement Units

Follow these steps to change the unit setting:

### Step 1: Open Machine Settings

1. Turn on the embroidery machine.
2. Enter the **Settings** menu.
3. Find the **User Parameter** option.
4. Find Embroidery Assistant Parameters
5. （page 3）Display unit

### Step 2: Select the Measurement Unit

Choose your preferred unit:

- **Inch**
- **Metric**

Confirm the selection to save the change.

### Step 3: Restart the power.Verify the Setting

After changing the unit:

- Check the embroidery area size.
- Confirm hoop dimensions.
- Verify that displayed measurements match your preferred unit.
